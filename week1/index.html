      "It's really hard to design products by focus groups. a lot of times, people don't know what they want until you show it to them." Steeve Jobs - Apple
      1. Immediate, Relevant, Fictional
      2. Reachability Matters
      3. Speed Matters
        1. Reduce image usage through sites/apps
        2. SVG/Webfonts are lower bandwidth
        3. Optimize and Minify CSS and JS files
        4. GZIP files t eliminate unnecessary data
